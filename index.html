About Us

Caffiend is an indie game development team formed within the creative environment of Algonquin College as part of their final project.

Our team of 11 passionate members is dedicated to creating a unique gaming experience that reflects our collective skills and vision. The name reflects our energy and determination, embodying the spirit that drives our work as aspiring developers.

Collaboration and continuous learning are at the heart of our work. We’ve built a website to showcase our progress, share our creative journey, and connect with the gaming community.

This platform is more than just a project—it’s a testament to our hard work and the culmination of everything we’ve learned throughout our time at Algonquin College.

Unique Buff Combinations

Each buff ties to a flavorful element—like Spicy for fire attacks or Salty for ice defenses—leading to unexpected synergies. Adapt, experiment, and master these dynamic power-ups to dominate the pantry!

Fast-paced Action

High-energy combat where quick reflexes and sharp strategy are key. With dynamic enemies, fluid movement, and ever-changing buff combinations, every battle is a thrilling test of skill. Dodge, strike, and adapt in the heat of the pantry.

Replayability

Randomized levels, dynamic buff combinations and new unlockable recipes, ensuring no two playthroughs are the same. Adapt to shifting strategies as new buffs reshape your playstyle.
