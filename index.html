About Us

Caffiend is an indie game development team formed within the creative environment of Algonquin College as part of their final project. Our team of 11 passionate members is dedicated to creating a unique gaming experience that reflects our collective skills and vision. The name reflects our energy and determination, embodying the spirit that drives our work as aspiring developers.

Collaboration and continuous learning are at the heart of our work. We’ve built a website to showcase our progress, share our creative journey, and connect with the gaming community. This platform is more than just a project—it’s a testament to our hard work and the culmination of everything we’ve learned throughout our time at Algonquin College.

🎨 Jada Gordon
